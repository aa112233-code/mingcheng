About Wildlife Guardians

We are a global non-profit organization dedicated to protecting wildlife and preserving biodiversity through science, conservation action, and education.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2008, Wildlife Guardians International works across six continents to protect endangered species and their habitats through innovative conservation strategies.

Our approach combines scientific research, community engagement, policy advocacy, and direct conservation action to address the most pressing threats to wildlife.

We believe that by protecting wildlife, we safeguard the health of our planet and ensure a sustainable future for all living beings.
